Free Site Audit

A practical first look at what is blocking leads on your website.

The free site audit is for local service businesses that want a clearer website, stronger local search foundation, better AI search readiness, or cleaner lead tracking. You get a practical read, not a recycled scorecard.

Request the auditSee the process

What gets reviewed

  • Conversion path and mobile CTA clarity
  • Service and service-area content
  • Indexing, schema, and internal links
  • GA4/Search Console lead visibility
What you receive

A short list of fixes, ordered by business impact.

The audit looks for problems that can stop a ready customer from calling, requesting a quote, booking, or sending a form. It also checks whether Google can understand the main pages and whether analytics can show real lead actions.

If the first fix is simple, the audit says that. If the site needs a deeper rebuild, the audit explains why and which pages should come first.

Good audit questions

  • Can a mobile visitor act without hunting?
  • Do service pages answer what customers ask before contacting?
  • Is the service area clear and honest?
  • Do schema and FAQ markup match visible content?
  • Are calls, forms, bookings, and quote paths tracked?
Not a magic report

What the audit will not do.

North Atlas keeps the audit useful by avoiding claims that cannot be supported.

No ranking guarantees

The audit can identify stronger foundations for search visibility. It cannot promise a number-one ranking, map-pack placement, or a specific AI search appearance.

No fake proof

If the site has no reviews, photos, case studies, or tracking data yet, the audit treats that as a trust gap instead of inventing proof.

No bloated scope

The recommendation starts with the page or path most likely to affect leads. Sometimes that is a rebuild. Sometimes it is copy, schema, tracking, or a better contact path.

FAQ

Questions before you request an audit.

Is the free site audit a full SEO campaign?

No. It is a first review that identifies the strongest next fixes. A full campaign or rebuild only starts if the scope makes sense after the audit.

Do you need access to my website or analytics?

Not for the first look. Public pages are enough to start. Search Console, GA4, or CMS access helps only if you want a deeper review.

Will the audit promise rankings or AI placements?

No. North Atlas Studio reviews the foundations that affect visibility and conversion, but does not promise rankings, map-pack placement, AI Overview inclusion, or AI Mode citations.

Ready for the first look?

Send your site and the main lead action you care about: calls, quotes, bookings, appointments, or forms.
