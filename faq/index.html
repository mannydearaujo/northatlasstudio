Do you build new sites or improve existing ones?

Both. North Atlas Studio can rebuild a site from scratch or improve the pages that already matter. The right path depends on the current structure, conversion gaps, and whether tracking is already in place.

What counts as a real lead?

A real lead is an action with business intent: a phone tap, completed form, quote request, booking click, or appointment request. Pageviews and scroll depth can help diagnose behavior, but they are not headline leads.

Do you optimize for AI-assisted discovery?

Yes, carefully. North Atlas Studio follows the same foundations Google recommends: helpful crawlable content, clear services and locations, internal links, structured data that matches visible content, and complete business information. It does not promise AI placements.

Can you guarantee a number one ranking or AI Overview placement?

No. No agency controls Google rankings, map pack placement, AI Overviews, or AI Mode citations. North Atlas Studio controls the quality of the site foundation, content structure, conversion path, and tracking.

What types of businesses do you work with?

North Atlas Studio is built for local, owner-operated service businesses such as contractors, home services, pet services, cleaning, landscaping, and similar businesses that rely on calls, quotes, bookings, or appointment requests.
