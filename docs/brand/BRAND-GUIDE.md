# NorthPoint Digital — Brand Guide v1

## Brand idea
**Data. Discovery. Direction.**

NorthPoint Digital should feel like a serious local-growth technology partner, not a generic marketing shop. The brand should communicate clarity, measurement, and forward motion.

## Logo
Use the filled cyan north-arrow mark. Avoid hollow/cutout versions for the primary mark.

Preferred lockups:
1. Primary horizontal: mark + `NorthPoint Digital`
2. Stacked: mark above wordmark
3. Icon-only: mark alone for favicon/avatar/app use

## Typography
- Headings: Inter Tight. Optional future upgrade: Space Grotesk.
- Body: Inter.

## Visual motifs
- Subtle dark grid
- Thin cyan data-flow lines
- Rounded dashboard cards
- Analytics cards, lead metrics, search/location icons
- Glow accents sparingly — do not make it look like crypto/gaming.

## Messaging system
Primary tagline options:
- Websites. Local SEO. AI Discovery. Lead Tracking.
- Data. Discovery. Direction.
- Websites that turn local searches into leads.
