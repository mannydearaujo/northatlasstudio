# E-E-A-T Voice Rule

Favor genuine operator voice over generic category copy. Show first-hand knowledge of how this
specific business actually works, what it sees day to day, and what makes it different from
competitors. Real specifics build experience, expertise, authority, and trust; filler and stock
phrasing erode them. Avoid AI-tell phrasing and clichés — "nestled," "look no further," "peace of
mind," "when it comes to" — and write the way the owner would actually talk. If a `client-voice-pack`
exists for this client, write from it; if not, run `client-voice-pack-builder` first.
