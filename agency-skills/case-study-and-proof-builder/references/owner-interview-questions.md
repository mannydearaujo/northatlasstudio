# Owner Interview Questions

Ten minutes by phone or text. Goal: their words, not yours — the answers become the case study's voice and the testimonial raw material.

1. Before we started, what was the most frustrating part of how customers found you (or didn't)?
2. What made you finally decide to do something about the website/Google presence?
3. Was there a moment you noticed something had changed? What happened?
4. What do customers say now when they call or come in — anything new about how they found you?
5. What surprised you about the process?
6. If another [trade] owner asked whether this was worth it, what would you tell them?
7. Anything you'd want other business owners to know about working with me?

Notes:
- Record exact phrasing; mark the lines that could become the testimonial and ask permission for those specific lines.
- If an answer contains a measurable claim ("phone rings way more"), check whether GA4/GBP data supports quantifying it — if not, keep it as a quote, not a stat.
