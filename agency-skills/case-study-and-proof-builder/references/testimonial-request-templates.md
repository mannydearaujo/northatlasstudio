# Testimonial Request Templates

Rules: Manny sends these personally. Ask for honesty, not positivity (review-gating rules apply to testimonials too — see `compliant-review-engine`). Make it a 2-minute ask. Always get explicit permission to publish with name/business.

## Text/SMS version
> Hey [name] — I'm putting together a short write-up about the work we did on [the website / your Google presence]. Would you be up for giving me 2–3 honest sentences about how it went and whether it's made a difference? I'd publish it with your name and business — totally fine to say no or keep it anonymous.

## Email version
Subject: Quick favor — 2 sentences about the [project]

> Hi [name],
>
> I'm building out case studies for NorthPoint and the work we did together is one I'd like to feature. Could you share 2–3 honest sentences on: what was frustrating before, and what's different now?
>
> I'd love to include your name and [business name], but anonymous works too if you prefer. And if anything about the project didn't land right, I'd genuinely rather hear that directly.
>
> Thanks — Manny

## Follow-up (one, after ~5 days, then drop it)
> No pressure on the write-up — if it's easier, I can draft something from what you told me on [date] and you just edit/approve it. Want me to do that?

(If they take the draft option: write it ONLY from things they actually said, send for approval, and keep their approval message.)
