# Prospect Scoring Rubric

Score 0-100. Treat the score as sales prioritization, not proof of poor performance.

- Missing website: +25
- Website exists but appears outdated, thin, slow, broken, or not mobile-friendly after review: +20
- GBP/Maps listing has reviews but site has weak conversion path: +15
- No clear call, quote, booking, or contact path on website: +15
- No visible service/location page structure: +10
- No apparent tracking signals or measurable lead path: +10
- Strong niche fit for North Atlas: +10
- Local competition looks stronger in site clarity or GBP activity: +5

Priority bands: 80-100 A, 60-79 B, 40-59 C, 0-39 low fit. Use evidence. If the website was not reviewed, say `site_not_reviewed`.
