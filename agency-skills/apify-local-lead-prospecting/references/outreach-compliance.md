# Outreach Compliance Guardrails

This is not legal advice. For commercial email, follow FTC CAN-SPAM basics: accurate From/Reply-To/routing information, non-deceptive subject lines, clear commercial identification when needed, valid physical mailing address, clear opt-out, prompt opt-out handling, and monitoring of any sender acting on your behalf. Source: https://www.ftc.gov/business-guidance/resources/can-spam-act-compliance-guide-business

Treat SMS and automated calls as consent-sensitive and higher risk. Default to manual email outreach unless the user explicitly provides a compliant consent workflow and sending system.

Use public/scraped data for relevance only. Do not imply a relationship that does not exist.
