# Offer Sheet Builder

Match prospect need to audit, rebuild, local pages, tracking, or retainer. Write deliverables, outcome, price band, proof, and next step.

**This is the client-facing deliverable.** When an `ai-search-readiness-audit` dashboard exists, treat
it as internal source material only — pull the findings and the suggested price band from it, but never
copy its internal pricing exploration, margin notes, or raw audit detail into the offer sheet. The
output here is the polished pitch the client actually sees: the chosen offer, the price, the scope, and
proof. See the "Internal vs client-facing deliverable rule" in the root `CLAUDE.md`.

## Workflow

1. Confirm the client/business context and source material (including any internal audit dashboard).
2. Read the references in this skill before producing final output.
3. Produce a concise, owner-facing deliverable plus implementation notes where useful — translate
   internal findings into client-safe language; don't paste internal notes in verbatim.

## Guardrails

Never promise rankings, map-pack placement, AI Overview inclusion, AI Mode citation, or guaranteed lead volume. Use verified facts and plain-English owner-facing recommendations. Keep this deliverable client-safe: no internal pricing strategy, margin reasoning, or audit minutiae the client doesn't need.

**Logo rule:** any offer sheet, proposal, or pitch summary that carries NorthPoint Digital's own branding must use the filled cyan north-arrow/compass mark from `assets/brand/svg/northpoint-mark.svg` — never a hollow/cutout mark or a cyan square tile with a letter. See `docs/brand/BRAND-GUIDE.md`.
