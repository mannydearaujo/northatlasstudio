# Offer Stack

## The four offers

**1. Free Site Audit** — the lead magnet, never sold standalone.
Full AI Search Readiness diagnostic run internally (`ai-search-readiness-audit`); the prospect receives a concise findings summary, not the internal dashboard. Purpose: open the conversation with evidence. Positioning line: "We ran your site through the same checks Google's own guidance describes — here's what's costing you calls."

**2. Conversion Website Build / Rebuild — $1,500–$3,500**
Band guidance: ~$1,500 = small site (≤8 pages, existing branding, no location cluster); ~$2,500 = standard (services + FAQ + tracking + schema + a starter location set); ~$3,500 = full rebuild with location cluster, photography coordination, migration/redirects. Always itemize what's included: pages, schema, GA4/GTM lead tracking, launch QA, Search Console setup, GBP alignment.

**3. Local SEO + AI Search Pages — $250–$500/page or bundled**
Location/service cluster pages on an existing decent site. $250 = template-driven page within an existing cluster; $500 = new-cluster pages needing research + owner verification of local detail. Bundle pricing for 5+ pages. Anti-doorway quality is the differentiator — say so.

**4. Growth Retainer — $300–$800/mo**
$300 = maintenance + GBP cadence (2 posts/wk) + review ops + monthly report. $500 = + 1 blog/mo + quarterly page additions. $800 = + 2 blogs/mo + ongoing CRO iteration + priority support. Every tier includes the weekly/monthly plain-English report (real leads, not vanity metrics).

## Packaging rules
- Lead with the problem the audit found, then the ONE offer that fixes it. Don't menu-dump all four.
- Natural ladder: Free Audit → Build/Rebuild → Retainer. Pages offer is the wedge when the site is decent but thin.
- Price anchoring: state what the deliverable replaces ("a $3,500 rebuild vs losing every after-hours caller to the next result").
- Every offer sheet ends with ONE next step and a time-bound response window (not fake scarcity — a real "this quote is good for 30 days").

## No-guarantee language (required, verbatim-adaptable)
"We don't promise rankings or AI-search placement — nobody honestly can. We build the structure, proof, and tracking that make you eligible and measurable, and you'll see exactly what's happening in plain-English reports."
