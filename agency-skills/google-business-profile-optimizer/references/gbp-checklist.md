# GBP Checklist

Static completeness: business name, primary category, secondary categories, phone, website, address/service area, hours, services, service areas, description, booking/contact links.

Engagement activity: recent reviews, owner replies, real recent photos, recent posts, Q&A, messaging/chat only if the business can respond.

Website alignment: services, NAP, service-area pages, and conversion path should match GBP.
