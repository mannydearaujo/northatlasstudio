# City Service Cluster Builder

Create local SEO page clusters that are useful enough to deserve indexing.

## Workflow

1. Confirm real services, service areas, and business capacity.
2. Read `references/location-page-guardrails.md`.
3. Use `references/keyword-cluster-template.md`.
4. Use `references/local-detail-verification.md` for every local claim.
5. Use `references/doorway-risk-checklist.md` before finalizing.

## Guardrails

Do not make near-duplicate pages that only swap city names. Do not invent drive times, landmarks, neighborhoods, routes, or local claims.
